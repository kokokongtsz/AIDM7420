About Me

Co-founder@Mimikaki

Koko is a dedicated professional with a diverse background in communication and entrepreneurship. Currently, she is the founder of Mimikaki, an ear treatment clinic that offers exceptional ear care and hearing health services. Since its establishment in 2020, Mimikaki has served over 2000 satisfied customers and received widespread recognition from the media. It has been featured in renowned publications and broadcast outlets such as RTHK, HKET, Ming Pao, AM730, and Yahoo News, etc.

Journalist

Prior to her venture into the healthcare industry, Koko worked in the communication field, gaining valuable experience as a financial journalist at Apple Daily Limited and Tasty Money. During this time, she conducted extensive research and analysis on various business topics, including finance, technology, and entrepreneurship. She covered breaking news, important events, and produced in-depth features and investigative reports on major business stories. Additionally, Koko served as an Account Associate at Occasions PR & Marketing Ltd., where she developed and executed successful PR campaigns for clients in luxury, art and culture, and non-profit sectors. She demonstrated her writing skills by drafting and editing press releases, media pitches, and other communication materials. Koko also managed client relationships and coordinated media interviews, securing coverage in top-tier publications.

Master Student

Currently, Koko is pursuing a Master's degree in AI and Digital Media at Hong Kong Baptist University. She is passionate about data analysis and seeks opportunities to further develop her skills in this field. Koko holds a Bachelor's degree in Social Science with a concentration in Financial Journalism from HKBU.
